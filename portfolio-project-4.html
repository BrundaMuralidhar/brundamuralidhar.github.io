The Residence of Harmony

The challenge was to mask the massing and planning of old and new to emerge into a building profile of oneness.

Their requirement was to design a 3bhk attached to their existing 4 bhk building in semi-traditional architectural style with emphasis on central courtyard, skylights and meditation room, evoking a sense of harmony by using local materials and transcending spatial setup through connectivity.
